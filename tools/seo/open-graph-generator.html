About Open Graph Generator

Open Graph meta tags control how your content appears when shared on social media:

  • Create meta tags for Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn, and other platforms
  • Customize title, description, and image for social sharing
  • Live preview of how your content will appear
  • Support for multiple languages and content types
  • Includes Twitter Card meta tags
