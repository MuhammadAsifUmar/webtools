About Unit Converter

This tool helps you convert between different units of measurement:

  • Length: meters, kilometers, centimeters, inches, feet, yards, miles
  • Weight: kilograms, grams, milligrams, pounds, ounces
  • Temperature: Celsius, Fahrenheit, Kelvin
  • Area: square meters, square kilometers, hectares, acres, square feet
  • Volume: cubic meters, liters, milliliters, gallons, quarts, pints
