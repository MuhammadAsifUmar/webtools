About Word Counter

This free online word counter provides instant character count & word count statistics for a given text. The tool reports the number of words, characters, sentences, and paragraphs in your text, along with estimated reading time.

How to use:

  1. Simply paste your text into the text box above
  2. Your word count and other statistics will update automatically
  3. You can also type directly into the text box
